What is TechConnect?

A free ICT Training Program

TechConnect: Palangoy

A community program that gives out-of-school youth in Barangay Palangoy the chance to learn computer skills that can open doors to better opportunities. Through friendly, hands-on training, participants learn how to use a computer, navigate the internet, communicate online, and work with basic software skills that can help them apply for jobs, start small businesses, or continue their studies.

The heart of the program is simple: to help young people feel confident in using technology so they can take steps toward a brighter future and be part of a community that’s ready for the digital world.

Functionality

  • Digital Skills Training – Hands-on lessons in basic computer operations and internet use.
  • Job Readiness Support – Equips participants with tech skills needed for employment.
  • Accessible Community – Conducted within Barangay Palangoy to remove travel barriers.
  • Practical Learning – Real-life tasks so learners can apply skills immediately.
  • Supportive Environment – Encourages learning at one’s own pace in a friendly setting.

Benefits

  • Employment Opportunities – Opens doors to jobs requiring basic computer literacy.
  • Empowerment – Builds confidence to engage in the digital economy.
  • Pathway to Education – Serves as a foundation for advanced ICT training.
  • Economic Uplift – Enables participants to explore freelancing and online selling.
  • Community Engagement – Encourages active participation via digital platforms.
  • Bridging the Divide – Ensures no youth is left behind in the technological era.
