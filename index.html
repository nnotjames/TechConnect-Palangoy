Why we created this web?

We created this web application to make attendance tracking simpler, faster, and more reliable for schools and organizations. Traditional methods like manual roll calls or paper based logs are time consuming and prone to errors. Our goal is to provide an efficient, user-friendly platform that streamlines the attendance process, reduces administrative workload, and ensures accurate record-keeping. By leveraging modern web technologies, we empower educators, staff, and parents to focus more on what truly matters: learning and collaboration.
